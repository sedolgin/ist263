Sarah Dolgin's Biography

I am a senior dual major in newspaper and online journalism and information management and technology with a concentration in data analytics.I am interested in merging my passion for reporting with my knack for data exploration to tell comprehensive stories about the world.

I have experience interning and freelancing with different kinds of professional media publications — from small nonprofit newsrooms to statewide outlets.I am also the executive editor of the premiere student-run arts and culture magazine on campus and spend time working on the communication school’s in-house digital news site as the special projects lead producer.
